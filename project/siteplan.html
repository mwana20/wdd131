Site Name

Rinah Library

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Rinah Library website is to provide an informative and engaging platform for book enthusiasts to explore a diverse collection of books, research materials, and online resources. The site will serve as a comprehensive resource for library services, book recommendations, membership registration, and digital resources. Our goal is to connect readers with valuable literary materials while fostering a love for learning and research.

Scenarios

  • How can I become a member of Rinah Library?
  • What books and resources are available in the library's collection?
  • Can I borrow books online or reserve a book in advance?
  • Does Rinah Library offer research assistance or study rooms?
  • Are there any upcoming library events or book clubs?
  • Does Rinah Library provide digital resources and e-books?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4B0082 (Indigo)

Secondary Color: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown)

Accent Color: #FFD700 (Gold)

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins - For clear, modern headings that convey professionalism and clarity

Body Font: Liter - For easy readability of book descriptions and research content
