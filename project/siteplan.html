Mubbala Phillip - Hiking Site Plan

Site Name

MWanaweika Hikings

Site Purpose

The purpose of the MWanaweika Hikings website is to provide an informative and engaging platform for hiking enthusiasts to discover and book guided hiking experiences. The site will serve as a comprehensive resource for trail information, safety guidelines, equipment recommendations, and booking services. Our goal is to connect nature lovers with experienced guides while providing essential information about various hiking trails and outdoor adventures.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a guided hiking tour with MWanaweika Hikings?
  • What hiking trails are available and what are their difficulty levels?
  • What equipment do I need for different types of hikes?
  • Are there group discounts or special seasonal hiking packages?
  • What qualifications do your hiking guides have?
  • Do you offer custom hiking experiences for private groups?

Color Schema

Secondary Color: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown)

Typography
